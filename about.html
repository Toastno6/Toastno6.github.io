Ben Booy (pronounced 'boy') is a British born photographer who now resides in the wonderland that is Massachusetts. When he's not knee deep in British car parts he can be found pointing a camera at anything and everything including, frequently, his friends. Most of them are still his friends so he must be doing something right. He enjoys the nickname 'Toast' which is derived from decades of competitive under-achievment.
